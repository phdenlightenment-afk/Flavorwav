The Idea

What if your straw could do more than just carry liquid from cup to mouth? What if it could carry vitamins, energy, probiotics — and taste incredible doing it?

How It Started

Turning Water Into Something More

FlavorWav™ started with a 40oz tumbler, a problem, and an obsession. Staying hydrated was a chore. Supplements were messy. Pre-workout was chalky. There had to be a better way.

We spent months developing a paper-based infused straw that dissolves functional ingredients — vitamins, caffeine, probiotics, and natural flavors — directly into your drink as you sip. No powders, no pills, no prep.

The result is FlavorWav™ — a line of 12" infused straws designed to fit your Stanley, Yeti, RTIC, or any 30oz and 40oz tumbler. Just insert, sip, and ride the wav.

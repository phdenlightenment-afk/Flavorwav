Our Story

Built for Real Tumbler Users

FlavorWav is a California-based company built for people who actually use their large-format tumblers every day. We design and manufacture large-format replacement straws for Yeti, Stanley, and similar tumblers.

Each straw is lined with great-tasting flavors and optional functional ingredients like prebiotic fiber, vitamins, probiotics, and other nutraceuticals.

Drop a FlavorWav straw into your tumbler, add your drink, and sip. The lining dissolves as you use it — no scooping, mixing, or mess.
